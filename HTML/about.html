ABOUT US

Preserving Our History

In the heart of a bustling city, TakeAlittle was born from a simple yet profound idea , the belief that in the hurried pace of modern life, people often overlook the beauty in the smallest moments. Founded in 2010 by visionary entrepreneurs, Zenitsu Cole and Inosuke Evans, TakeAlittle started as a small boutique with a big dream to become the best there is.
