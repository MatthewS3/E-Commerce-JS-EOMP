TakeAlittle is not just a store, it's an embodiment of a dream, a vision to redefine the shopping experience. Rooted in the belief that even the smallest moments deserve attention and care, TakeAlittle aspires to become the epitome of excellence in the retail world. ABOUT US

Preserving Our History

In the heart of a bustling city, TakeAlittle was born from a simple yet profound idea , the belief that in the hurried pace of modern life, people often overlook the beauty in the smallest moments. Founded in 2010 by visionary entrepreneurs, Zenitsu Cole, Tanjiro Stark and Inosuke Evans, TakeAlittle started as a small online commercial enterprise with a big dream to become the best there is.

Our Team
What Is TakeAlittle

TakeAlittle is a India-based website dedicated for sharing stock photography under the Pico license. Pico allows photographers to upload photos to its website, which are then curated by a team of photo editors. In Pico we are aspiring to be one of the largest photography suppliers on the internet.
